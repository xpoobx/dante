Websites About Protected Areas

Protected areas are designated areas intended to house and protect nature. Protected Areas serve to promote biodiversity, protect endangered species, and conserve cultural values and nature. The existence of protected areas is paramount in protecting nature, as they impose strict restrictions on human interference. With the ever-expanding reach of humanity, protected areas stand between nature and destruction.
